WORDS OF WONDER

You must have read a lot about the importance of writing for your readers.

While this is a good thing to do, it is also true that in order to capture an audience for your blog, you need to write for yourself first.

Your blog your personal space , and it reflects your identity and your individual voice .

You might not capture thousands of fans in a day, in a month, or even in a year, but if your blog post is helping even one person, its creation has been worth your time.

Just stick to your niche and write anything that interests you within that niche.

There is always a motive behind blogging.
